About Us

Bangladesh's Premier Overseas Recruitment Agency

AR Group Recruitment Agency is a leading overseas manpower recruitment firm based in Dhaka, Bangladesh. With over 15 years of experience, we specialize in connecting skilled and unskilled Bangladeshi workers with reputable international employers across the globe.

Licensed by the Bureau of Manpower, Employment and Training (BMET), we operate with full legal compliance and maintain the highest ethical standards in the recruitment industry.
